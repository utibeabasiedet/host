EXCLUSIONS

The following are excluded from all plans: -

  1. Inpatient services including but not limited to accommodation and feeding.
  2. In-Hospital General and Specialist Consultations
  3. Management of Chronic Diseases including but not limited to consultation, prescription drugs and laboratory tests
  4. Viriliy enhancing drugs, Herbal drugs, non-prescription drugs, vitamin supplements, food supplements and experimental drugs and treatment
  5. Other Laboratory and Radiological Investigations not listed in the schedule of covered services.
  6. Consultations with unrecognized consultants, hospitals, family doctors, therapists, dental practitioners or complementary medicines practitioners
  7. Any other diagnosis, treatment, service, procedure or investigation not listed in the schedule of covered medical service
CONDITIONS
